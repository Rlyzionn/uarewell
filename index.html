About Susan

Passionate About Inspiring Others

Hi, I'm Susan — a Master Practitioner and Trainer of NLP and Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist with over 23 years of practice. I specialize in PTSD, chemical dependency, trauma, and personality disorders, blending evidence-based therapy with spiritual wisdom.

I hold a master's certification in Reiki and teach people to connect with their higher selves through meditation and hypnosis. I am deeply familiar with manifestation, goal attainment, and guiding people to receive what they seek from the Universe.

My certification in Huna, expertise in NLP, Critical Debriefing, and Intervention enables me to meet clients exactly where they are — from teens 12+ to the most complex cases in correctional facilities.

I am high energy and stop at nothing to help others heal — it is my deepest passion.
